Reviews page

Save any reviews to the reviews attribute; content and length don't matter.

Process the request and pass reviews in the context by the key "reviews" to the template defined in file "book/templates/book/reviews.html".

By default, Django is looking for templates in all of your " <application_name>/templates" directories, so we do not include " book/templates" in the template path, because Django will do it for us.
